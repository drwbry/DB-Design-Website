Our Story
Baking is our love language.

Sweet Crumb started in a farmhouse kitchen in 2011 when Maria Chen began selling sourdough at the local farmers market. What started with one table and two loaves has grown into a beloved neighborhood cornerstone — but the recipe hasn't changed.

Every item on our menu is made in-house, every morning, by a small team that genuinely loves what they do. We source from six local farms and two regional roasters.

— Maria Chen, Founder
